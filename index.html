Games that I have completed in 2023

I don't have many hobbies, and after running into some work related issues earlier this year (i.e. getting laid off), I've regressed into what I was during high school: a loser that only plays video games. So! For this site I've decided to log how many games I have played through, or remember playing through, this year.

    Here is the list of games:
  • The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom
  • Final Fantasy I-V
  • Final Fantasy IX
  • Theatrhythm Final Bar Line
  • Overwatch 2

The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Starting off on a high, this is the big one folks. This is probably the best game I've played this year, or at least the best new game, and certainly the most memorable one. This game was addictive, it's a good thing I finished it weeks back because it was overtaking me. I couldn't get away from it. It consumed me. So y'know it was pretty good I guess.

Final Fantasy I-V

I'm grouping all these together because I'm both lazy and economical. I played through the first five numbered Final Fantasy games earlier this year and I'm inclined to say that it was a mistake. Not that the games were bad, gentle reader, just that they are so similar to each other that I needed to space these out way more to prevent burnout. Alas, burnout was not prevented. Definitely taking a break before I jump into VI, the last one I haven't played yet.

Final Fantasy IX

I've been on a Final Fantasy kick for the past year so this also gets included in this list. This one was okay for the most part but was also kinda boring. I hate being bored when playing games the most, so the slow pace of battles didn't endear me to the game unfortunately.

Theatrhythm Final Bar Line

Lo and behold, another Final Fantasy game on this list. This one is a rhythm game, a genre that I am quite fond of but not particularly good at. I would say that this would be my #2 of the year with the main complaint being just how daunting the song list is. There are over 500 songs in total to play through and, suffice to say, it is a lot of music to get through.

Overwatch 2

I play this because it's the only game that both me and my friends play. I don't have much to say. It's not good. Maybe I need more friends in addition to more hobbies...
